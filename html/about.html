Profile

Nepal Machine is one of prominent engineering marketing companies in Nepal with strong presence in the form of 2 Branches at present and corporate office at Kathmandu, Nepal.

Established in the year 2024, NM is a financially sound and professionally managed organization. NM has been promoted by leading Business houses, Industrial conglomerate & Institutions. NM has been able to establish its presence as leading industrial and construction Machine supplier in Nepal. Our team from different department has been built to provide sales, service and spares (3S) support in machines with assurance in quality from different countries.

NM is a customer-centric company, with a single-minded focus on customer satisfaction for machines. Its priority is to build long term client relationships, with complete customer satisfaction as the most important non-negotiable objectives.

Our Vision and Mission

Since establishment of constitution in Nepal in 20-September 2015, Nepal has been enlisted as one of the fast growing countries. After this, Rapid development in field of construction and industries can be seen.

With the motive to bring country to economic prosperity and provide facility to our customer, we establish good relationship with manufacturer from different countries and Nepal as well. This makes the ease of business to foreign and Nepalese manufacturer under single platform, so that our customer gets different industrial and construction purpose machines, under single roof.

Our aim is to become the most reliable source which offers world class engineering machines with quality assurance in product.
